King's Head

 

About

The King's Head is a family run business in Lampeter, Ceredigion. It is a friendly student orientated pub in a small market town in mid-rural Wales. Come and relax with Mal, Lesley, and the dogs Kath & Alf and enjoy the beautiful scenery of rural Wales.

We are only 20 mins from the coast (and only 30 minutes from popular seaside resort New Quay). Taste the local food of Lampeter, fresh from the farms, explore the rural Welsh countryside, go to local towns such as Aberaeron (famed for it's fishing) or visit town's such as Aberystwyth and Carmarthen. Bus routes are available in town to many places.
